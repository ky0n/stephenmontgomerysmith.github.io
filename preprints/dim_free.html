Nakhlé Asmar and Stephen Montgomery-Smith, On a weak type (1,1) inequality for a maximal conjugate function. Studia Math. 125, (1997), 13-21. In a celebrated paper, Burkholder, Gundy, and Silverstein used Brownian motion to derive a maximal function characterization of $H^p$ spaces for $0 < p < \infty$. In this paper, we show that their method extends to higher dimensions and yields a dimension-free weak type (1,1) estimate for a conjugate function on the $N$-dimensional torus.
