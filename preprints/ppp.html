Stephen Montgomery-Smith, The \(p^{1/p}\) in Pisier's Factorization Theorem. Proceedings of Conference on Geometry of Spaces at Strobl, Ed: P.F.X. Müller and W. Schachermayer, L.M.S. 1990. We show that the constants in Pisier's factorization theorem for \((p,1)\)-summing operators from \(C(\Omega)\) cannot be improved. (tex, dvi, ps, pdf.)
