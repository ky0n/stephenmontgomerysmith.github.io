Stephen Montgomery-Smith, The Distribution of Rademacher Sums. Proc. A.M.S. 109 (1990), 517-522. We find upper and lower bounds for \(\Pr\left(\sum \pm x_n > t\right)\), where \(x_1, x_2, \dots\) are real numbers. We express the answer in terms of the \(K\)-interpolation norm from the theory of interpolation of Banach spaces. (tex, dvi, ps, pdf.)
